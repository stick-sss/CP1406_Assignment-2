ABOUT ME

We are a small family-run local business. In 2012, We owned this bakery in Kirwan.We specialise in traditional Australian baked goods.Our baking methods are first in the industry.Our baker is from Paris, where he has been trained to bake at the top of the line.Different from traditional bakeries, we pay more attention to the hygiene and safety of products and the buying experience of customers.Our shop assistants can help you select and recommend the best selling products of the season.We will give customers a taste test of each season's best-selling product before you buy it.Our bakery is located at 123 Prize Lane in Kirwan, Queensland.Next to the bakery is the popular Willows Shopping Centre.We have many famous customers, such as James Cook University, The Cowboys and Telstra.
